The guy who shows up

Owner-operated out of Bonduel for 20+ years

Behind Lucky 7 is Matt Torphy. He answers the phone, he drives the flatbed, and he’s spent more than twenty years pulling neighbors out of ditches, snowbanks, and plain bad days — on every county road, fire lane, and back forty between Shawano and the state line.

Some calls are a dead battery in a driveway. Some are a classic Morgan that has to arrive exactly as it left. Either way, your vehicle rides like it’s his own.

One outfit, two names: the truck doors say Lucky 7 Transport, most folks around town say Lucky 7 Towing. Same clover, same Matt, same number.
