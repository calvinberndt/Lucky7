About Lucky 7 Towing

For over 20 years, Lucky 7 Towing has been the trusted name in professional towing and roadside assistance services throughout rural Northeastern Wisconsin.

We proudly serve the communities of Shawano, Bonduel, Wittenberg, Navarino, Gresham, Pulaski, Cecil, Keshena, and Gillet and surrounding areas. Whether you're stranded on a country road or need vehicle recovery in town, our experienced team knows the local area and is ready to help 24/7.
